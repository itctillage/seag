The Sea Goodness App

Dedicated to get information for seafood and how to prepare them

Fish and shellfish is a nutrient-rich food containing high quality protein, long-chain polyunsaturated fatty acids (Omega-3) and important vitamins and minerals

Top Commonly Consumed Seafoods:

  • Shrimp
  • Canned Tuna
  • Salmon
  • Pollock
  • Tilapia
  • Catfish
  • Crab
  • Cod
  • Clams/Pangasius/Scallops

Features

  • Search for Seafood Species easily by common market name
  • Share your pictures through social media
